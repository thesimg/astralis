HOW TO PLAY: ASTRALIS

Made by thesimg

Welcome to the world of Astralis

Play


Use WASD or the arrow keys to move around the world

Press SHIFT to dash in the direction you are moving



You can stick to any side of a platform

Platforms that have the [!] symbol will vanish after you jump off of them

Platforms that have the [~] symbol are broken and have a limited lifespan

Normal platforms have no symbol



FODDIAN GAMEMODES
have no normal platforms, ascend the map as fast as you can, faster dash cooldown

The normal Foddian gamemode (top portal) is tall, wide, and fairly easy map

The Foddian Lite gamemode (left portal) is a smaller, wider, and faster map

The Foddian Extreme gamemode (right portal) is a taller, thinner, and more challenging map



TRAVERSAL GAMEMODES [DISABLED]
has a mix of all platforms, traverse the map to the east as fast as you can



You may enter a specific seed into the "seed" input, so you can compete against your friends on a specific map

Press [R] to restart the level

Press [C] to copy the link to that level to share with people
